How We Help

Dakota Harvest of Hope connects farmers and individuals facing stress, isolation, or mental health challenges with others who truly understand agricultural life.

  • Peer support from people with shared experiences
  • A safe space to talk and be heard
  • Connection without judgment
  • Community-driven support (not clinical treatment)

Important Information

This organization provides peer support and community connection. We are not a licensed medical or mental health provider.

If you are in immediate danger or experiencing a crisis, please contact local emergency services.
